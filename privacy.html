Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to products developed by Beardmachine Games. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This page is here to inform you about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or information from users of Beardmachine Games products. No data is transmitted to any remote server or third party.

Local Data

Any data or settings used by Beardmachine Games products are stored locally on your device. This includes things like game progress, preferences, or high scores. You can delete this data by uninstalling the game.

Third-Party Services

Beardmachine Games products do not use any third-party services, such as analytics, advertising, or social media integrations, that would collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Since no data is collected at all, Beardmachine Games products are safe for players of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to the way Beardmachine Games products handle data, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any future versions will be made available within the game or on our official website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at beardmachinegames@gmail.com.
